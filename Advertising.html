Advertising

Advertising is the business of attracting people's attention to a product or service. It is the public promotion of a product, service, cause or movement. Promotion is done through advertising through the media – TV, Internet, newspapers, radio, trade magazines, movie theaters or billboards. Additionally, advertisements appear in store windows, restaurant placemats, playgrounds, on the sides of vehicles, and on the sides of buildings. Many companies opt for outdoor neon signs to promote their business.
