warehousing service

Warehousing is the act of storing goods that will be sold or distributed later. While a small, home-based business might be warehousing products in a spare room, basement, or garage, larger businesses typically own or rent space in a building that is specifically designed for storage

We provide service of warehouse If any seller wants to sell by keeping it in our warehouse, then we provide this service. Advantages of keeping products in warehouse

1- No charge is taken from the seller for keeping the product in the warehouse, only the packing charge will be taken for the product which is sold.

2 - If your product is available in our warehouse then our team will operate your entire account from packing to delivered

3 - By keeping in the warehouse, many problems of the seller are solved, as the seller does not need to update the inventory again and again.
